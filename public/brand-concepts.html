Kahani Indian Street Food

Three directions for review — Edinburgh, Scotland  ·  2026

01

The Heritage

A premium, award-winning identity. Deep maroon with antique gold letterforms and ornamental rule details evoke the richness of Indian craft and the warmth of the restaurant. Well-suited to printed menus, signage, and formal event materials.

Best for: menus  ·  signage  ·  events  ·  gift vouchers

Deep Maroon · Antique Gold · Cream
02

The Modern Flame

Clean, contemporary street-food energy. A stylised tandoor flame in saffron sits alongside a bold dark teal wordmark, separated by a vivid accent bar. Versatile across digital screens, social media, takeaway packaging, and outdoor signage.

Best for: social media  ·  online ordering  ·  packaging  ·  outdoor

Deep Teal · Saffron · Soft Teal
03

The Story

Kahani means "story" in Hindi and Urdu. This concept builds the brand around that meaning — a K monogram seal on the left, elegant italic script for the name, and the line "a story worth tasting." Layered and narrative-driven; ideal for events, catering proposals, and high-end print.

Best for: events & catering  ·  packaging  ·  premium print  ·  website hero

Deep Navy · Antique Gold · Warm Cream
